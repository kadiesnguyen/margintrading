P2P DEPOSIT AND WITHDRAW

P2P (peer-to-peer) deposit and withdrawal is the process of transferring money directly between individuals or organizations without going through any other intermediary. In the cryptocurrency context, P2P deposits and withdrawals are often used to buy and sell cryptocurrencies between users directly, without the intervention of an intermediary exchange. This helps reduce transaction costs and time, while increasing safety and benefits for people
